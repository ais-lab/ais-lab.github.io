We participated in the 26th SICE Symposium on System Integration (SI2025)!
Ten members of the Lee Lab presented their research findings.
Two of them are senior undergraduates (B4) who took on the challenge of presenting at an academic conference!
We also had the opportunity to interact with researchers from other universities, making it a highly stimulating and inspiring event.
